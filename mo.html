The health and safety of the consumer was at the heart of the decision to establish the Type Approval Labs.

The request for standardisation is one that the authority has always taken seriously and pursed vigorously over the years.This is largely because the issue of substandard devices and its impact on consumers is one that the authority concider critical.

It is for that purpose that the authority established the Type Approval Labs to protect the health and safety the consumer.We needed to ensure that electronic Communication equipment used on the Ghanaian market are safe,and to also ensure our people that the telecommunication space is safe.

It is particularly thrilling for us that on the continent,we are one of the few regulators who have established this lab and the International Telecommunication Union (ITU) is partnering with us to trian oth regulators on the continent and across the world.

The NCA is in the process of drafting Type Approval Regulations to streamline type approval activities. This is the first time that is being done and we are happy to be executing our mandate to the benefit of the industry, government and consumers especially.
